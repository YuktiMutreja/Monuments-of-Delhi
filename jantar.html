JANTAR MANTAR

Jantar Mantar comprises from two words : Yantra meaning instruments and mantra meaning formulae was constructed in 1724. Maharaja Jai Singh of Jaipur who built this observatory went on to build other observatories in Ujjain , Varanasi and Mathura.

Jai Singh had found the existing astronomical instruments too small to take correct measurements and so he built these larger and more accurate instruments.

  • VISITING TIME : 9:00 a.m to 7:00 p.m

  • ENTRY FEE : Rs 5 per head for citizens of India

  • NEAREST METRO STATION : PATEL CHOWK AND RAJIV CHOWK
